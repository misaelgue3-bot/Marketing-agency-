Frequently asked questions.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. We earn your trust with results, not contracts that tie you down. We only ask for 30 days' notice to wrap things up cleanly.

Is ad spend included in the price?

The plan price covers our work managing your ads. The budget itself you pay directly to Google or Meta — we never mark it up. Most clients start with $100–$400 a month.

How fast will I see results?

A new website and optimized Google profile usually bring more calls within the first month. Local SEO builds over 3–6 months. Ads can bring customers within days.

What if I already have a website?

Great — we'll review it for free. If it's solid, we build on it. If it's holding you back, we'll explain why and what a fix would cost. No pressure.

Do you work with businesses in other cities or countries?

Yes. Everything works remotely — we have clients across the US and Latin America who've been with us for years. Calls, reports and approvals all happen online.

Who owns the website and accounts?

You do. Your domain, your website, your social and ad accounts — all registered in your name. If you ever leave, everything stays with you.
