Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. We keep your business by earning results, not by locking you in. We only ask for 30 days' notice to wrap things up cleanly.

Is ad spend included in the price?

The plan price covers our work managing your ads. The ad budget itself is paid by you directly to Google or Meta, so it's 100% transparent — we never mark it up. Most clients start with $150–$500/month in ad spend.

How fast will I see results?

A new website and optimized Google profile usually produce more calls within the first month. Local SEO builds over 3–6 months. Ads can bring customers within days. We'll give you honest timelines in your free plan.

What if I already have a website?

Great — we'll audit it for free. If it's solid, we'll build on it. If it's holding you back, we'll tell you why and what a fix would cost. No pressure either way.

Do you work with businesses outside your city?

Yes. Everything we do works remotely — we've got clients we've never met in person who've been with us for years. Calls, reports and approvals all happen online.

Who owns the website and accounts?

You do. Your domain, your website, your social and ad accounts — all registered in your name. If you ever leave, everything stays with you.
